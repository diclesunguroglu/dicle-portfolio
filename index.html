Profile

I approach every project as a problem to understand before anything else. Not just how it looks, but why it exists, what is not working, and where things break down. I bring a calm, structured way of thinking into teams, breaking complexity into clear parts and turning ambiguity into direction.

I think in systems, not just screens. Starting simple, making decisions early, and shaping ideas into something intentional and usable. The goal is always the same. Solve the right problem and make the solution feel effortless.
